● Key conclusions or insights.
We can see that high blood pressure is more common than low blood pressure among the patients.
March 2024 recorded the highest number, with three visits.
Older groups tend to have higher blood pressure than younger ones, and males generally have higher values than females
We can confirm that blood pressure is higher in patients with a diagnosis of 'hypertension' or 'high blood pressure
